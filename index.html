ABOUT US

Nurturing Global Leaders through Curriculum embedded learning.

The Rathinam Global Deemed to be University (RGU), established under the prestigious Rathinam Group of Institutions, is a future-focused institution located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Built with a vision to transform higher education through innovation, interdisciplinary learning, and industry integration, the university creates an ecosystem where academic excellence meets real-world experience. Situated within the vibrant Rathinam Techzone Campus, the university offers students a unique learning environment that combines academics, technology, research, entrepreneurship, and industry exposure under one ecosystem. The institution is accredited with an A++ Grade by NAAC. Rathinam Global Deemed to be University is committed to delivering quality education supported by modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, advanced laboratories, research facilities, and industry-oriented academic practices. The university offers 100+ programs across multiple disciplines through its diverse academic departments and specialized schools. With 20K+ students, 100+ Programmes, 120+ Startups Incubated, 3Cr PA Highest Placement Package, 50+ Centres of Excellence, 100+ Global Collaborations, Rathinam Global continues to nurture an engaging and progressive academic culture.
